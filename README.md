# 8x8 fudge brownie recipie

### ingredients

* 2 eggs (room temp)
* 1 cup of sugar (maybe another 1/3)
* 2/3 cup maida
* 1/2 cup cocoa powder
* 1/2 cup oil
* vanilla essence
* salt

### steps

1. set preheat
2. oil pan
3. warm oil and mix cocoa powder in it
4. blend eggs and sugar for 3-4m, in 30s intervals
5. add vanilla and oil+cocoa mixture to blender, mix for a minute
6. take out blender mixture in a bowl
7. add maida and salt to bowl
8. fold until no maida visible, dont overmix
9. pour in pan, spread, sprinkle salt, tap a few times
10. bake for 15-20m on low flame, take out, tap a few times, put back in
11. bake another 15-20m
