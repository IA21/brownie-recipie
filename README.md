# 8x8 fudge brownie recipie

### ingredients

- 3 eggs (room temp)
- 1.5 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup maida
- 2/3 cup cocoa powder
- 2/3 cup oil
- vanilla
- salt

### steps

1. set preheat
2. oil pan (only oil paper?)
3. warm oil and mix cocoa powder in it
4. blend eggs, vanilla and sugar
5. pour egg+sugar mixture to oil+cocoa mixture, mix mix mix
6. add maida and salt to bowl
7. fold until no maida visible, dont overmix
8. pour in pan, spread, sprinkle salt, tap a few times
9. bake for 20m on low flame, take out, tap a few times, put back in
10. bake another 15-20m
